▶ FAQ

QUESTIONS WE GET A LOT.

When will I start seeing results? +

SEO takes time — anyone who tells you otherwise is lying. Most clients start showing up in local search within 4-8 weeks. The Google Business profile usually kicks in faster, sometimes within days. The website itself starts working immediately — leads can find you and contact you the day it goes live.

What if I already have a website? +

Is it showing up on Google Maps when people search for your service nearby? That's where the calls come from. Most websites we see aren't optimized for local search — they're basically digital business cards nobody can find. We fix that.

What's the missed call text back? +

When a customer calls and you can't answer — because you're on a job — they automatically get a text back within seconds saying you'll get back to them. No more lost leads because you were busy working. Included in the Pro plan.

Do I need to do anything? +

Not really. Fill out our onboarding form, answer a few questions about your business, and we handle everything — website, Google Business, SEO, all of it. You just keep doing the work and answer the calls that come in.

What if I want to cancel? +

No contract, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime. We're confident enough in our work that we don't need to trap you. If you're not happy, you shouldn't stay — simple as that.

Why do I need Google? I already run social media ads. +

Social media puts you in front of people who weren't looking for you. Google captures people who are actively searching for exactly what you do, right now, ready to pay. When someone Googles "pressure washing near me" at 10pm they have their credit card out. If you're not showing up, you don't exist to them.

Why spend money on a website when word of mouth is already working? +

Word of mouth is great — until it isn't. One slow season, one dry spell, and you're scrambling. Google works 24/7 even when referrals dry up. Even your word of mouth referrals Google you before they call. A website doesn't replace word of mouth — it makes it work harder.

Is $297 really all I pay? +

Yes. No setup fee, no hidden fees, no contract. $297/month covers everything — website, hosting, Google Business optimization, unlimited edits, all of it. One job a month from Google pays for the whole thing. The Pro tier at $397 adds a review funnel, missed call text back, automated lead follow-up, and a multi-page website. Same deal — no contract, cancel anytime.
